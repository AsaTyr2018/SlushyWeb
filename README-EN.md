# SlushyWeb

Welcome to **SlushyWeb** - a recipe collection for hot days, chilled glasses and seriously good frozen drinks.

## Open SlushyWeb

### [https://asatyr2018.github.io/SlushyWeb/](https://asatyr2018.github.io/SlushyWeb/)

From bright fruit classics and creamy milkshakes to tropical recipes inspired by Indonesia, SlushyWeb combines adventurous ideas with recipes designed for frozen-drink machines.

## Discover the collection

SlushyWeb includes:

- refreshing fruit slushies and alcohol-free frozen cocktails
- classic, vegan and unusual milkshakes
- ingredients and instructions in German and English
- flavor profiles for sweetness, acidity and creaminess
- allergen, cold-chain and machine compatibility guidance
- a country filter for realistic ingredient availability

## Find what works for you

Search the collection, browse categories, select your country and decide whether specialty shops or rare ingredients should be included. SlushyWeb then highlights the recipes that best match the ingredients available to you.

Availability information is intended as guidance. Product ranges, formulations, import rules and possible allergen traces may change. If you have allergies, always check the packaging of the products you actually use.

Enjoy mixing, chilling and serving.
